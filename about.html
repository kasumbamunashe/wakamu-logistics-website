Company Overview

Wakamu Logistics is a leading provider of comprehensive logistics and supply chain solutions, dedicated to efficiently managing the intricacies of transportation and distribution for businesses across diverse industries.

With a foundation rooted in professionalism and operational excellence, Wakamu Logistics offers end-to-end logistics services, including freight forwarding, warehousing, transportation management, customs clearance, and supply chain consulting.
